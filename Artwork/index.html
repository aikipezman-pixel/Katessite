Artwork

Rooted in land, story, and spirit,
each piece is a dialogue between
the seen and the unseen.

This artwork explores the relationship between human experience and the more-than-human world.

Through layered textures, natural pigments, and symbolic motifs, I create visual narratives that speak to themes of transformation, belonging, and the sacred.

Influenced by mythology, ecology, and personal journey, my work invites reflection, connection, and a deeper remembering of our place within the earth’s story.
